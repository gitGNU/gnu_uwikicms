Soap box

I'll never repeat it enough: UWiKiCMS fits exacly my needs, and it's a very specialized solution. Do not expect it to make coffee any day, or that it will have this or that fonctionnality. Should you convince me that a given feature actually needs to be implemented, it could still never make it to the source tree. UWiKiCMS is the result of my conception of on-line content.
